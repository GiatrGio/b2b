About the Estate

Founded in 1948 in the heart of the Peloponnese, Kalamata Gold has remained a family-owned operation for three generations. We specialize in single-origin Koroneiki olives, harvested by hand and cold-pressed within 4 hours to maintain the lowest possible acidity levels. Our partnership with Aegean Wholesale brings our award-winning exports directly to your kitchen.
